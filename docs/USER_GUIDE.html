1Open & install the app

SkillForge runs right in your web browser — there's nothing to download from an app store during the pilot.

Open the link you were sent in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android / desktop). That's it — you're in.

Add it to your home screen (recommended)

For an app-like, full-screen experience that launches from your home screen:

  • iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button → Add to Home Screen → Add.
  • Android (Chrome): tap the ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen / Install app.
⚠️ Important during the pilot Your progress is saved only on this device, in this browser. There is no account and no cloud sync yet. If you switch browsers or devices, or clear browsing data, your progress won't follow automatically — so back it up (see section 9).

2Your privacy choice

The very first time you open SkillForge, you'll see a privacy banner. We ask before collecting anything.

SkillForge can collect anonymous usage stats to help us improve the pilot — for example, which screens get used. This is strictly opt-in:

  • Tap Allow anonymous stats to help us out, or No thanks to keep everything off.
  • We never send your name, email, handle, or anything you type — only a random anonymous ID.
  • Until you choose, nothing is tracked at all.
  • You can change your mind anytime in Settings → Anonymous Analytics.

Tap Read our Privacy Policy → in the banner to see the full details first.

5Log your work — the core loop

This is the heart of SkillForge. XP comes from building, not watching. Every real thing you create earns XP.

Tap the big purple + in the center of the tab bar, then:

  • Pick what you made — Project, Cert, GitHub, Book, Script, Design, Reflect, Event, or Other. Each is worth different XP.
  • See where it counts — a banner shows which skill the output advances, and whether it completes the skill for a bonus.
  • Add a title & what you built / learned — be specific; this becomes your proof-of-work.
  • Add a key takeaway (optional) for a little extra XP.
  • Submit — watch the +XP float up. If it completes a skill, you'll get a milestone celebration. 🎉
✅ The loop Learn → Build → Log Output → Gain XP → Unlock Milestone → Share → Repeat. Logging real work is what moves your ring.

9Your data & privacy controls

You're in full control. Open Profile → ⚙️ Settings and scroll to Data & Privacy.

  • Anonymous Analytics — flip this toggle on or off anytime. Off means absolutely nothing is sent.
  • Export Data — download a backup file of all your progress. Do this regularly!
  • Import Backup — restore your progress from a backup file (e.g. on a new device or browser).
  • Privacy Policy — read exactly how your data is handled, in plain language.
  • Reset All Progress (Danger Zone) — permanently erases everything from this device. Can't be undone.
🔒 The short version Your progress lives only on your device. There's no server, we can't see your data, and nothing you log is shared. Because of that, exporting a backup is the only way to protect your progress against clearing your browser or switching devices.

10Tips & FAQ

How do I earn XP fastest?
Log real outputs — certs and projects are worth the most, and completing a skill grants a big bonus. Adding a key takeaway gives a little extra.

What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak has a short grace period, and you can spend a Freeze from Settings to protect it. Hit 7, 14, and 30-day streaks for milestone bonuses.

Why can't I see other real users?
The Community tab is a preview during the pilot. Sample builders show the experience; real sharing comes in a later release.

I cleared my browser / switched phones and lost everything!
Progress is device-local with no cloud sync yet. Always Export Data first, then Import Backup on the new device. Without a backup, cleared data can't be recovered.

How do I turn analytics off?
Settings → Data & Privacy → toggle Anonymous Analytics off. Done.
