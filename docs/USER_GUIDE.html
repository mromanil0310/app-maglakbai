2Your privacy choice

The very first time you open MaglakbAI, you'll see a privacy banner. We ask before collecting anything.

MaglakbAI can collect anonymous usage stats to help us improve the pilot — for example, which screens get used. This is strictly opt-in:

  • Tap Allow anonymous stats to help us out, or No thanks to keep everything off.
  • We never send your name, email, handle, or anything you type — only a random anonymous ID.
  • Until you choose, nothing is tracked at all.
  • You can change your mind anytime in Settings → Anonymous Analytics.

Tap Read our Privacy Policy → in the banner to see the full details first.

5Log your work — the core loop

This is the heart of MaglakbAI. XP comes from building, not watching. Every real thing you create earns XP.

Tap the big purple + in the center of the tab bar, then:

  • Pick what you made — Project, Cert, GitHub, Book, Script, Design, Reflect, Event, or Other. Each is worth different XP.
  • See where it counts — a banner shows which skill the output advances, and whether it completes the skill for a bonus.
  • Add a title & what you built / learned — be specific; this becomes your proof-of-work.
  • Add a key takeaway (optional) for a little extra XP.
  • Submit — watch the +XP float up. If it completes a skill, you'll get a milestone celebration. 🎉

6The Evolve milestone map

Evolve shows your whole journey as a connected map of skill milestones.

  • ✓ Completed skills are filled in green with the date you reached them.
  • In-progress skills show a progress bar and how many outputs remain.
  • 🔒 Locked skills unlock automatically once you finish their prerequisites.
  • My Roadmaps — set a Priority Focus, enroll in multiple paths, or tap + Add to build a custom roadmap.
✏️ Editable roadmaps On any custom roadmap, tap the ✏️ Edit chip next to the milestone map to add, rename, reorder, or remove milestones — but only before you log your first proof. Once the journey starts, the structure locks in so your XP target stays stable. To restructure a started roadmap, use Delete & Rebuild in the ⋯ menu (your XP and logged proof are always kept).
🎓 Prove you know it — knowledge challenge When you complete a skill, you can take an optional knowledge challenge: 10 multiple-choice questions drawn from verified, reliable sources (official docs, standards bodies, trusted references). Score 7 out of 10 to earn +100 XP and the ✓ Validated badge on that skill. Every answer reveals an explanation and its source — so you actually learn, not just guess.

9Cloud backup & sign-in

Sign in once to keep your progress safe across every device and browser. No password needed.

Open Profile → ⚙️ Settings → Cloud Backup and enter your email address. We'll send you a Magic Link — a one-tap sign-in link that arrives in your inbox.

  • Tap the link in the email — it opens the app and signs you in automatically.
  • Your progress syncs to the cloud instantly.
  • Open the app on any other device, sign in the same way, and your progress is there.
🔒 Do this first Sign in via Cloud Backup right after onboarding. It's the single most important step to protect your progress — clearing your browser or switching devices won't lose anything once you're backed up.

The Magic Link expires after 1 hour. If you need a new one, just tap Send Sign-in Link again.

10Data & privacy controls

You're in full control. Open Profile → ⚙️ Settings and scroll to Data & Privacy.

  • Anonymous Analytics — flip this toggle on or off anytime.
  • Export Data — download a backup file of all your progress.
  • Import Backup — restore your progress from a backup file.
  • Privacy Policy — read exactly how your data is handled, in plain language.
  • Reset All Progress (Danger Zone) — permanently erases everything from this device. Can't be undone.
🔒 The short version Once you sign in via Cloud Backup, your progress is stored in the cloud and on your device. Without signing in, it's device-local only. Either way, we can't see the content of what you log — only anonymous usage stats if you've opted in.

11Tips & FAQ

How do I earn XP fastest?
Log real outputs — certs and projects are worth the most. Completing a skill grants a big bonus, and passing its Prove you know it knowledge challenge (7/10) adds +100 XP. Adding a key takeaway gives a little extra. Signing in via Cloud Backup protects all of it.

What's the "Prove you know it" challenge?
After you complete a skill, you can take an optional 10-question quiz built from verified, reliable sources. Get 7 or more right to earn +100 XP and a ✓ Validated badge on that skill. Each answer shows an explanation and its source, and you can retake it if you fall short.

What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak has a short grace period, and you can spend a Freeze from Settings to protect it. Hit 7, 14, and 30-day streaks for milestone bonuses.

Why can't I see other real users?
The Community tab is a preview during the pilot. Sample builders show the experience; real sharing comes in the next release.

I cleared my browser — is my progress gone?
If you signed in via Settings → Cloud Backup, your data is safe in the cloud — just sign in again on the new browser or device and it will sync back. If you didn't sign in, use Import Backup to restore from an exported file. Without either, cleared data can't be recovered — so sign in first!

What is a Magic Link?
It's a passwordless sign-in. Enter your email → get a one-tap link in your inbox → tap it → you're signed in. No password ever created or remembered. The link expires after 1 hour.

Can I customise my roadmap?
Yes — for custom roadmaps, tap the ✏️ Edit chip before you start logging. You can add milestones, rename them, and reorder them. Once you've logged your first proof the roadmap locks in to keep your XP target stable.

How do I turn analytics off?
Settings → Data & Privacy → toggle Anonymous Analytics off. Done.
